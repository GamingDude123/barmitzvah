About Me

I am Jack and as I become a bar mitzvah, I am very glad to be sharing my story with you to the public. On each website page, there is something that is included that is either about me, or something related to me and my bar mitzvah. As a 12 almost 13-year old, my maturity continues to grow as a whole. My hobbies are coding, playing sports, video games and reading and writing. Over the past couple of years, I went from coding in Arduino (another coding software), to publishing websites to the public. Outside of the coding world, I really enjoy playing basketball, ping-pong, and tennis. If I could rank each of these 3 sports, I would say that tennis is number 1, ping-pong is number 2 and basketball is number 3.
